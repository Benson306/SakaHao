Saka Hao

Saka Hao is a combination of two sheng words 'Saka' and 'Hao'. 'Saka' is a sheng word for 'search' while 'Hao' refers to a 'house/appartment'.

The purpose of this site is to enable its users to post and search for houses in their areas of choice.

This system was created most especially for students who seek to live out of school but within the area around the school. It also provides landlords with a platform to showcase their properties and find tenants.
